Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our community members

Dental Services

Comprehensive dental care including cleaning, checkups, tooth extraction, and oral health consultations.

Pregnancy Care

Complete prenatal care, regular checkups, ultrasounds, and guidance throughout your pregnancy journey.

Baby Vaccine

Essential immunizations for infants and children following the Department of Health vaccination schedule.

Pampurga

Traditional herbal medicine and natural health remedies for digestive health and overall wellness.

Anti-Tetanus

Tetanus vaccination and booster shots for wound prevention and protection against tetanus infection.

Flu Vaccine for Seniors

Specialized influenza vaccination programs designed for senior citizens and elderly community members.

General Check-up

Routine health examinations, vital signs monitoring, and preventive health assessments for all ages.
